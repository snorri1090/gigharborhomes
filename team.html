Our Team...
Linda Petersen, of Keller Williams Realty in Gig Harbor, is more than just a real estate agent, she is an expert in the field of homes and luxury properties. With over 22 years of real estate experience, Linda has handled every imaginable type of transaction. her goal is always to have a win-win situation for both buyers and sellers.

Keller Williams Realty is ranked as one of the top real estate companies and third largest in the nation, and Petersen is one of the reasons why. She has received awards for being top listing agent, top selling agent and top sales team. She is a member of the National Board of Realtors, Master Builders Association, National Home Builders Association, Northwest Multiple Listing Service, Institute of Residential Marketing, Accredited Buyer Representative and the Chamber of Commerce.

Lida has also enjoyed raising her daughter Lauren, who is currently attending the University of Washington Business School. They enjoy tennis, skiing, sailing, golfing, swimming and the Gig Harbor lifestyle.

Linda's team includes Debra Branch, a licensed assistant Buyer's Agent and marketing/transaction coordinator with over 10 years experience. Together they are now focused on a successful "shift" in the real estate industry, and move ahead with great optimism as real estate continues to be a great investment.

Before joining as part of the ownership group of Keller Williams Realty in Gig Harbor, Linda had 12 years experience leading the real estate department at Canterwood Golf & Country Club. She supports many charities such as Habitat for Humanity, Boys & Girls Club, Basket Brigade and Children's Home Society.

Her continued goal of providing award winning service with honesty, integrity and knowledge is just one of the reasons that her referral business continues to grow.

"I want to thank all our wonderful clients for their trust and confidence in the past years," Linda says. "As always, a referral is the highest compliment one can ever receive."
